Privacy Policy for WakeQuake

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Overview

WakeQuake ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

WakeQuake does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults) and includes only:

  • Your countdown duration preference
  • Your shake sensitivity setting
  • Your best record score
  • Whether you've seen the tutorial

Data Storage

All settings and data remain on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

WakeQuake does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

WakeQuake may request the following permissions:

  • Motion & Fitness: To detect shake gestures
  • Notifications: To display local notifications (optional, can be disabled in settings)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the App Store.
