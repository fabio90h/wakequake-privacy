Privacy Policy

Overview

AwakeQuake ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

AwakeQuake does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults) and includes only:

  • Your countdown duration preference
  • Your shake sensitivity setting
  • Your best record score
  • Whether you've seen the tutorial

Data Storage

All settings and data remain on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

AwakeQuake does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

AwakeQuake may request the following permissions:

  • Motion & Fitness: To detect shake gestures
  • Notifications: To display local notifications (optional, can be disabled in settings)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Support & Frequently Asked Questions

How does AwakeQuake work?

AwakeQuake keeps you awake through progressive challenges. Tap the screen 3 times or shake your phone during each round. If you don't respond, the app will vibrate (warning phase) and then sound an alarm (alarm phase).

Can I use AwakeQuake with headphones?

Yes! The alarm sound will play through your headphones so you won't disturb others.

Does AwakeQuake work in the background?

Yes, AwakeQuake continues to function even when your screen is off or while using other apps.

How do I adjust the settings?

Tap the gear icon in the top right corner to access settings where you can adjust countdown duration and shake sensitivity.

The shake detection isn't working properly

Try adjusting the shake sensitivity in settings. Choose "Gentle" for easier detection or "Earthquake" for harder detection.

How do I reset my record?

Go to Menu (gear icon) → Statistics → Reset Record

App not making sound
  • Check that your device is not in silent mode (use the side switch)
  • Ensure your volume is turned up
  • Try restarting the app
Vibration not working
  • Make sure vibration is enabled in your device settings
  • Check that your iPhone is not in Low Power Mode, which may affect vibration
